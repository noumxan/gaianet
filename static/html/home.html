UK Online Safety Bill: A New Era of Accountability

2021
First draft of the Online Safety Bill introduced
2023
Bill received Royal Assent and became law in September
2024
Major provisions begin taking effect

Key Provisions:

  • Tech companies must protect users from harmful content
  • Platforms need proactive systems to identify and remove harmful material
  • Heavy fines for non-compliance (up to 10% of global turnover)
  • Criminal liability for senior managers who fail to protect users
