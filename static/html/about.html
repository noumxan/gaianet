About GaiaScan

What is GaiaScan?

Our tool helps detect harmful content, including racism, and provides an easy way to report cybercrime. It also distinguishes between AI-generated and real images, ensuring users can navigate online material safely.

Problem, Action and Solution

Many people, particularly from marginalised groups, find it difficult to identify harmful or racist content online. Our tool detects harmful material, explains it in five languages, identifies whether images are real or AI-generated, and helps users report cybercrime, offering support to process distressing content calmly.

Our Mission

At GaiaScan, we are committed to creating a safer online environment for everyone. We believe in leveraging technology to empower users to navigate digital spaces with confidence and awareness. Our mission is to make harmful content detection accessible, educational, and actionable.
